About

page is designed for use with Discord, Steam and other apps that do not support the Steam Browser Protocol.


How to use this page:

https://prefortress.com/connect/?(IP or Domain):(Server port)/(Server password)

Examples:

  • https://prefortress.com/connect/?192.168.0.1:27015
  • https://prefortress.com/connect/?mycoolserver.example:27015
  • https://prefortress.com/connect/?192.168.0.1/password

This page is open source!
