About

This page is designed for use with Discord, Steam and other apps that do not support the Steam Browser Protocol.


How to use this page:

https://prefortress.com/connect/?(IP):(Server port)/(Server password)

Examples:

  • https://prefortress.com/connect/?192.168.0.1
  • https://prefortress.com/connect/?192.168.0.1:27015
  • https://prefortress.com/connect/?192.168.0.1/password

Official Servers:

  • Standard 1 - https://prefortress.com/connect/?66.179.81.82:27015
  • Standard 2 - https://prefortress.com/connect/?66.179.81.82:27016
  • Custom Maps - https://prefortress.com/connect/?66.179.81.82:27017
