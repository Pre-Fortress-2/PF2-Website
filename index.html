About

Pre-Fortress 2 is a mod that aims recreate Team Fortress 2’s beta as seen in Trailers 1 and 2, the most proment returning beta feature is the return of grenades, both frag and class specific grenades such as scout’s concussion grenade and demo’s mirv pack. Other features include weapons like the nailgun and tranq, beta recreations of maps and various strange cut mechanics.
